About This AI

Securing the Future is an AI-inspired tool that helps users understand online identity risks such as data breaches and weak security habits. It simulates how AI can recognize risk patterns and give personalized safety tips.

This project focuses on prevention by encouraging strong passwords, private accounts, and two-factor authentication to help users protect their digital identity.
